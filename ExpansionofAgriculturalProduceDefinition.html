Broader Protection for Farmers

The act now covers an extensive range of products, ensuring more comprehensive protection against theft.

The definition of agricultural produce has been broadened to include bamboo and other items not previously covered. This extension is part of the Act's amendment, aiming to protect a wider range of agricultural products from theft.
